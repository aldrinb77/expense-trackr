About this app

This Expense Tracker helps you quickly log debits (expenses) and credits (income), see summaries, and keep an eye on your budgets and saving goals.

  • Account – Log in or create an account. Each account has its own data.
  • Dashboard – Date range filter + daily / weekly / monthly / yearly summaries.
  • Budgets – Monthly saving goal and per-category budgets with progress.
  • Charts – Category breakdown, last 7 days spending, and 90‑day net cash flow.
  • Transactions – Full list with filters, edit/delete, and CSV export.

Tip: use the date range filter and dark mode toggle in the header to customize the view.
